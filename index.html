    Welcome to Luxedoors

    Welcome to Luxedoors – Your Trusted Door & Window Installation Experts At Luxedoor, we specialize in secure, stylish, and reliable solutions for every entry point in your home or business. Whether you need internal or external door installations, window replacements, or internal and external fire-rated doors, our expert team delivers top-quality craftsmanship with a focus on safety, durability, and design.     Composite Doors Built to Last

    At Luxe Doors and Windows, Doors and Windows, we only install high-grade composite doors that combine strength, style, and security. Our doors are built using a solid timber core or Foam filled with a metal reinforcing frame for high-performance. advanced locking systems—giving you long-lasting durability, excellent energy efficiency, and peace of mind. hether you're upgrading for looks or improving home security, our composite doors offer the perfect balance of quality and performance

    Quality uPVC Doors & Windows

    Our uPVC doors and windows are designed for efficiency, security, and style. We use only premium-grade uPVC that won’t warp, fade, or crack—ensuring your home stays warm, quiet, and secure all year round. With a range of modern styles and finishes, our installations are low-maintenance, weather-resistant, and built to last. Whether you're replacing tired units or upgrading for better energy performance, we've got the perfect fit for your property.

    Sleek Aluminium Bifold Doors

    Bring the outside in with our premium aluminium bifold doors—perfect for creating open, light-filled spaces. Engineered for smooth operation and slim sightlines, our bifolds offer a modern, high-performance solution for both homes and commercial properties. Built from strong, lightweight aluminium, these doors are low-maintenance, energy-efficient, and available in a range of colours and finishes to suit your space.

    Certified Fire Doors for Maximum Protection

    At Luxe Doors and Windows, we supply and install certified fire doors designed to meet strict safety regulations. Our fire doors are tested and rated to provide critical protection in the event of a fire—helping to contain flames and smoke, and giving occupants valuable time to evacuate. Built with high-quality materials and fitted with intumescent seals and fire-rated hardware, our fire doors are ideal for both residential and commercial settings where safety is a priority. Trust us to install fire doors that not only look good but also help save lives.

    Stylish Internal Doors to Suit Every Home

    Upgrade your interior with our range of high-quality internal doors, designed to add style, privacy, and functionality to any room. Whether you’re after a modern minimalist look or something more traditional, we offer a wide variety of finishes, colours, and designs to match your décor. All our internal doors are built for durability, with smooth operation and solid construction you can rely on. From classic panel doors to contemporary flush styles, we’ve got something to suit every taste and budget.

    About me

    Hi, I’m Lea Ternent — the proud owner of Luxe Doors North East. I was born and raised in Alnwick, Northumberland, and now live in Morpeth with my fiancée and our three amazing kids. Everything I do, both in life and in business, is driven by pride, integrity, and the desire to do things right — first time, every time. Before launching Luxe Doors, I built a strong reputation running my highly rated locksmith business, Lockforce Northumberland. Through that work, I saw countless cases of poor-quality door and window installations — jobs where customers were left believing their door was supposed to “just be like that.” I knew things could be done better — and that’s exactly why Luxe Doors was born. My background also includes military service in the Commandos, where I learned the value of precision, discipline, and attention to detail — qualities I bring into every single project. At Luxe Doors North East, we’re not just selling doors and windows — we’re raising the standard for how they’re installed. I work alongside Alex, an expert joiner whose confidence, knowledge, and eye for detail set us apart from the rest. And best of all — we deliver customer service that is second to none. We take pride in being approachable, reliable, and professional from the first call to the final fit. When you choose Luxe Doors, you’re choosing quality, honesty, and a team that genuinely cares.
